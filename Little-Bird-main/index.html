About Us

Find out about our story as Easy Load!

We provide quality and cost-effective same-day transportation services.

  • Our Medical Courier Service specializes in delivering time-sensitive and life-saving medical supplies. We'll deliver pharmaceutical supplies, laboratory specimens, surgical instruments, and reports, as well as medical equipment and machine components, to pharmacies, laboratories, hospitals, and doctors' and dentists' offices.
  • Medical supply wholesalers, airports, institutions, enterprises, government agencies, and people all use our services to deliver products and documents.

Our medical courier service will convey your urgent fragile freight shipments that require particular care, temperature control, or extra security as well as your expedited and speciality medical shipments.
